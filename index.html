Diginex Organization
The South African Financial Freedom Initiative - an organization dedicated to giving citizens financial freedom by the courtesy of our partners & sponsers. Diginex is providing a monthly grant to qualified citizens. Click here here to learn more about Diginex.

Diginex Grant
The Diginex grant is received every month on the 15th by qualifying citizens who have applied for the grant. This grant will be issued every month until January 2027. To apply for the Diginex grant click here.
• Ages 18 - 24 receive R1500 per month
• Ages 25 - 35 receive R2500 per month
• Ages 36 - 49 receive R3500 per month
• Ages 50 - 61 receive R4500 per month
• Ages 62 - 90 receive R5000 per month
You qualify if:
• You have are a South African citizen
• You have no income or have an icome less than R10 000 per month
• You have a South African Bank account in your name or have a mobile number RICA'd in your name
You don't qualify if:
• You have a criminal record
• You are not a South African citizen
• You have an income more than R10 000 per month
How to apply?
To apply click here and follow the steps, a R90 once off application fee is required for background checks & administration. Please note that applications have 24 hours to be secured with the application fee. After that you'd have to reapply.
Our Partners & Sponsers
The grant is financed by our sponsers & partners which are a list of companies and organizations invested in the future & well being of South Africa. The intention is to recirculate the country's finances, so more peaople have access to basic needs & facilities to develop the country's social structure, and provide economic empowerment.
